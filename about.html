About

Hi. My name is David Jeffs and I am an aspiring UX/UI designer.

I hold both Engineering and Commerce degrees from the Australian National University

I am currently pursuing a Masters of Interaction Design degree at the University of Queensland.

I believe in transforming design inspiration from the traditional arts into todays technological pursuits. I am currently fascinated by Islamic architecture and decorations, and am intrigued by the detail contained within romantic landscape paintings.
